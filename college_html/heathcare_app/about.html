About Health Tracker

Health Tracker is a web application designed to help you keep track of your health and wellness. Our mission is to provide you with the tools you need to live a healthier life.

With Health Tracker, you can:

  • Track your daily symptoms
  • Receive personalized health tips
  • Contact healthcare professionals
  • Learn more about maintaining a healthy lifestyle
